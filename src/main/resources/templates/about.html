The Story of

the Electric Toothbrush Sisters

A virus came...

and all the schools closed.

Suddenly, parents were teachers

and home-schooling commenced.

Math remained important,

as well as reading and writing -

but music class became crucial.

A papa decided now was the time

to dust off the rickety piano

and learn how to strike the keys.

A Nina had many ideas to rattle out in melody.

In times of crisis

there must be joy.
